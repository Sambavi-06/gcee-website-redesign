A Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1984 under the Institute of Road and Transport by the Tamil Nadu State Transport Corporation, our institution was transferred to the Directorate of Technical Education (DoTE) in 2021.

Located on a sprawling lush green campus of 200 acres between Chithode and Bhavani, GCE Erode provides a congenial atmosphere for Technical Education. We are well known for our academic performance, research activities, and remarkable placements.
